Perks for merchants Stay with us · unlock more

Perks that reward merchants who stick around.

Payment processing is the foundation. Perks are the extras we turn on for merchants in good standing — starting with a turnkey branded gift card program at the six-month mark.

Flagship perk: six months of active processing in good standing unlocks gift cards. Human review before enablement — never auto-approved.

Featured perk

Your branded gift cards — no magnetic stripe required

Closed-loop cards for your store only. A card number on the back, PIN under scratch-off, balance lookup on our site, redemption at your counter.

Tokenized cards

Each card gets a unique ID and PIN. No mag stripe, no ISO encoding project — type or scan at the counter when you're ready.

Balance lookup

Customers check remaining value on our site with card ID + PIN. Gift card balance →

Physical or digital

Order plastic with your logo and our variable numbering, or start digital-only and add cards later. We help with the first print spec.

Your brand, your liability

You issue stored value to your customers; OmniTender provides the ledger and tools. We do not hold gift-card customer funds.

How the six-month perk works

01

Go live on OmniTender

Apply, get approved on partner rails, and start accepting payments. Gift cards stay locked until you hit six months active.

02

Month four — get ready

We reach out for logo, program name, and load amounts. Optional: quote plastic from our vetted print partners.

03

Month six — program live

After human review, we enable your dashboard tools and balance page. Sell cards at the counter; customers redeem with ID + PIN.

Common questions

Is this a Visa gift card?

No. These are closed-loop cards good only at your business — simpler, cheaper, and designed for local shops and boutiques.

Can I get gift cards on day one?

The perk unlocks at six months so we support merchants who commit to the relationship. Payment processing is available from go-live.

Who holds the money on the card?

You do, as the merchant issuer. OmniTender tracks balances and redemptions; settlement stays on your existing payment relationship.
