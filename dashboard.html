Employee Registration

Register Employee Account

Enter a temporary username and PIN, plus the employee registration passcode to commission your account. You will configure your MFA credentials upon first login.

The Employee Passcode is a one-time registration gate code set by your admin (backend env var). Your admin provides this — do not guess it. Once registered, use Log In with your starter PIN on future visits.

Activate Your Console Account

Choose your permanent username and PIN, then scan the QR in your authenticator app. The account will appear as OmniTender. Scan on another device, or copy the setup key.

Add to your authenticator app

    Your security keys

    Register a USB security key, Windows Hello, Touch ID, or device passkey for daily sign-in. Your authenticator app and recovery codes are backup when a passkey is unavailable.

    Sign in to manage keys.

    Tip: USB security keys are the most reliable daily login. Google Password Manager sometimes shows “could not verify user” — that usually means a passkey already exists for omnitender.us or Windows/Google verification was cancelled. Use your USB keys, or remove the old passkey in Chrome → Password Manager before trying again.
