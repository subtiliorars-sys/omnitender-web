By opting in, you agree to receive recurring automated operational and promotional text messages from OmniTender — including account, sales, onboarding, support, and promotional alerts — at the mobile number you provided. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

Message frequency varies.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Such charges are billed by and payable to your mobile service provider. OmniTender does not charge for the messages, but your carrier's standard rates apply.

How to cancel

You can cancel at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send STOP, we will send a final confirmation message and you will no longer receive texts unless you opt in again.
