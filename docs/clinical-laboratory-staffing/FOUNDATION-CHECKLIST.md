# Foundation Checklist — Before Your First Hospital Contract

Work through this list in order. Do not skip **credentialing** or **insurance** — hospitals will block you at the door without them.

---

## Phase 1 — Entity & identity (Week 1)

| Step | Action | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1.1 | Confirm LLC is **active** with NC Secretary of State | ☐ |
| 1.2 | Obtain / confirm **EIN** (IRS) | ☐ |
| 1.3 | Open **business checking** in OmniTender Systems LLC name | ☐ |
| 1.4 | File **DBA** if invoicing as a name other than "OmniTender Systems LLC" | ☐ |
| 1.5 | Register for **NC state tax** IDs if required (sales tax usually N/A for services; confirm with CPA) | ☐ |
| 1.6 | Engage **CPA** for estimated quarterly taxes (self-employment + income) | ☐ |
| 1.7 | Engage **healthcare/business attorney** for template review | ☐ |

---

## Phase 2 — Insurance & risk (Week 1–2)

| Step | Action | Minimum typical hospital ask |
|------|--------|------------------------------|
| 2.1 | **Professional liability (E&O / malpractice)** for MLS services | $1M per claim / $3M aggregate |
| 2.2 | **Commercial general liability (CGL)** | $1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate |
| 2.3 | **Cyber liability** (if EMR/LIS access) | $1M (recommended) |
| 2.4 | **Workers' compensation** or valid **WC exemption** | Varies — hospital vendor form will specify |
| 2.5 | Name hospital as **additional insured** on CGL when COI requested | Per their form |
| 2.6 | **Certificate of Insurance (COI)** template from broker | PDF ready to send |

**Tip:** Tell your broker you are an **independent contractor MLS** placing through your LLC at acute-care hospitals. Wrong classification on the policy can void coverage.

---

## Phase 3 — Professional credentials (Week 1)

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| ASCP MLS certification | Current; PDF from ASCP |
| State license(s) | Every state you will physically perform testing |
| Diploma / transcript | BS in CLS/MLS or equivalent |
| Resume (CV) | 10 years — highlight benches, instruments, LIS |
| Skills checklist | Hospital may provide; complete honestly |
| BLS/CPR | AHA; current |
| TB test / immunizations | Per hospital occupational health |
| Background check | FBI/state; hospital may rerun |
| Drug screen | Often within 30 days of start |
| References | 2 supervisory lab references |
| Driver's license | For ID badge |

Store in: `credentials/OmniTender-MLS-Credentialing-Packet.pdf` (updated quarterly)

## Phase 5 — Vendor onboarding (Per client)

Hospitals and MSPs (HWL, AMN, Medefis, HealthTrust, etc.) each have portals. Common requirements:

| Item | OmniTender document |
|------|---------------------|
| Vendor application | LLC info, EIN, banking for ACH |
| MSA / contract | Your MSA or their paper (attorney reviews their paper) |
| W-9 | IRS form |
| COI | From broker; meet their limits |
| HIPAA BAA | Yours or theirs (attorney compares) |
| SOC 2 / IT security questionnaire | Answer honestly; LLC likely has minimal infra |
| Joint Commission / CMS mock survey prep | Follow hospital lab policies |
| Conflict of interest / compliance attestations | Sign only if true |
| Direct deposit / remittance form | LLC bank account |

**If the hospital insists on their contract:** never sign without attorney review. Their paper often favors the hospital on payment terms, indemnity, and IP.

## Phase 6 — Billing operations (Before hour 1 on site)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 6.1 | SOW specifies **rate, guaranteed hours, OT, holiday, invoicing frequency** |
| 6.2 | Identify **billing contact** and **timesheet approver** (name + email) in SOW |
| 6.3 | Confirm **purchase order (PO)** number if required — no PO, no start (if their policy) |
| 6.4 | Set calendar reminders: invoice due date + late-fee trigger |
| 6.5 | Use [timesheet form](./templates/10-timesheet-verification-form.md) every week |
| 6.6 | Send invoice within **2 business days** of period end |

---

## Phase 7 — Ongoing compliance

| Frequency | Task |
|-----------|------|
| Weekly | Signed timesheet + invoice |
| Monthly | Reconcile AR; follow up at 7 days past due |
| Quarterly | CPA estimated taxes; credential expiry check |
| Annually | License renewal, ASCP CMP, insurance renewal, MSA renewal |
| Per incident | Report to insurer if any patient harm allegation or specimen error near-miss with liability exposure |

---

## Red flags — do not start work

- Verbal-only agreement ("we'll get paperwork later")
- Client refuses to specify **rate** or **guaranteed hours** in writing
- Client wants you as **1099** but controls schedule like staff with no contract
- Their contract has **unlimited indemnification** or **no payment deadline**
- They won't provide **vendor setup** or **PO** before you clock in
- **Personal guaranty** required from you (push back — LLC exists for a reason)
- Payment terms **Net 60+** with no premium rate (negotiate higher rate or walk)

---

## File retention (keep 7+ years)

- Executed MSA, SOW, BAA, amendments
- All invoices + proof of delivery (email read receipt)
- Signed timesheets
- Bank deposit records
- Email re: schedule changes, rate confirmations
- Credentialing submissions
