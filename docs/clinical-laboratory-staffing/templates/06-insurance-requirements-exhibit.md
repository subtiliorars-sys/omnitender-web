# EXHIBIT C — INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

**To Master Services Agreement — OmniTender Systems LLC**

---

Contractor shall maintain, at its sole expense, the following coverage during the Term and all SOW performance periods:

---

## 1. PROFESSIONAL LIABILITY (ERRORS & OMISSIONS / MALPRACTICE)

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Coverage | Claims-made or occurrence for professional laboratory services |
| Limits | **$1,000,000 per claim / $3,000,000 annual aggregate** |
| Retroactive date | No later than start of first SOW (or full prior acts if claims-made) |
| Tail | Extended reporting period if policy cancelled |

**Insurer:** A.M. Best A- VII or better (or hospital-approved equivalent)

---

## 2. COMMERCIAL GENERAL LIABILITY

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Limits | **$1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 general aggregate** |
| Coverage | Bodily injury, property damage, personal injury |
| Additional insured | Client named on certificate when required by facility vendor policy |
| Waiver of subrogation | Per Client form if required |

---

## 3. CYBER LIABILITY (RECOMMENDED / MAY BE REQUIRED)

| Requirement | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Limits | **$1,000,000** if accessing hospital EMR/LIS remotely |
| Coverage | Data breach, privacy liability, notification costs |

---

## 4. WORKERS' COMPENSATION

| Requirement | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| WC coverage | Per North Carolina law for LLC members/employees if applicable |
| **OR** WC waiver/exemption | Signed affidavit if Professional is sole member and exempt |
| Employer's liability | $500,000 if WC applies |

*Hospitals often require a specific WC form — obtain from Client vendor packet.*

---

## 5. AUTOMOBILE (IF APPLICABLE)

If Professional transports specimens or travels between sites on Client business:

- Hired/non-owned auto: **$1,000,000** combined single limit (or personal auto with business use disclosed)

---

## 6. CERTIFICATES

Contractor shall deliver **Certificate of Insurance (ACORD 25)** within **five (5) business days** of request showing:

- Policy numbers and dates
- Limits meeting this Exhibit
- Additional insured endorsement (CGL only) when required
- **30-day cancellation notice** to Client (endeavor to obtain)

---

## 7. NO DIMINUTION

Contractor shall not reduce limits or cancel coverage during an active SOW without Client's consent and replacement coverage.

---

## 8. PRIMARY / NON-CONTRIBUTORY

Contractor's professional liability is **primary** for professional services claims arising from Contractor's performance.
