## 4. FACTORS SUPPORTING INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

The Parties acknowledge:

1. **Separate business.** Contractor operates a distinct LLC with its own EIN, bank account, and business expenses.

2. **Specialized skill.** Services require ASCP certification, state licensure, and professional judgment not held by general staff.

3. **Limited duration.** Each SOW is for a defined term (typical 13–26 weeks), not indefinite employment.

4. **No employee benefits.** Contractor receives no Client health insurance, PTO, retirement, or unemployment coverage.

5. **Tax reporting.** Client receives Form **W-9** and reports payments on **1099-NEC** (if applicable); Client does not withhold income or FICA for Contractor.

6. **Right to other work.** Contractor may serve other clients when not conflicting with SOW schedule.

7. **Profit/loss risk.** Contractor bears business expenses, insurance, and non-payment risk.

8. **Professional control.** Professional exercises independent judgment in testing, QC interpretation, and technical decisions within CLIA and medical director framework — Client sets **what** tests are offered, not **how** professional standards are applied.

---

## 5. OPERATIONAL BOUNDARIES

**Client may:**
- Set shift schedule in SOW
- Require compliance with hospital policies, HIPAA, safety, and infection control
- Require competency assessments per lab standard
- Direct escalation of critical values per protocol

**Client may not:**
- Require Contractor to work exclusively for Client beyond active guaranteed hours without new SOW
- Discipline Contractor as an employee (may terminate SOW per MSA)
- Withhold taxes or provide W-2 to Contractor for Services under this Agreement (unless law requires reclassification)

---

## 6. SUBSTITUTION

Contractor may not send substitute personnel without Client's **prior written approval** and equivalent credentials. Client may reject unqualified substitutes without penalty.

---

## 7. RECLASSIFICATION

If any authority reclassifies Contractor or Professional as an employee, the Parties will cooperate in good faith; **Client remains obligated to pay all Fees** for Services performed through the reclassification date.
