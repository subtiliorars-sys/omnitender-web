## 1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"**Confidential Information**" means non-public information disclosed by either Party, including business plans, pricing, staffing models, credentialing documents, patient information (if any), and technical systems information. PHI is governed by HIPAA/BAA when applicable.

**Exclusions:** Information that is (a) public without breach, (b) already known, (c) independently developed, or (d) received from a third party without restriction.

## 2. OBLIGATIONS

Each Party shall:

- Use Confidential Information only to evaluate or perform a potential or actual business relationship
- Not disclose to third parties without prior written consent
- Protect with reasonable care (minimum same as own confidential data)
- Limit access to need-to-know personnel bound by similar obligations

If compelled by law, the receiving Party shall give prompt notice (if legal) and cooperate on protective order.
