# EXHIBIT E — NON-PAYMENT REMEDIES AND ENFORCEMENT

**To Master Services Agreement — OmniTender Systems LLC**

This Exhibit supplements MSA Section 5 and is **enforceable to the fullest extent permitted by North Carolina law**.

---

## 1. UNDISPUTED vs. DISPUTED AMOUNTS

**1.1 Undisputed** means Fees for Services with (a) executed SOW, (b) submitted timesheet, and (c) no written line-item dispute within five (5) business days.

**1.2** Client shall pay **100% of undisputed amounts** on Net 15 even if a **portion** of an invoice is disputed.

**1.3** Disputes must cite **specific dates, hours, and reasons** in writing to [CONTRACTOR BILLING EMAIL].

---

## 2. LATE PAYMENT INTEREST

Overdue sums accrue **1.5% per month** (18% per annum), calculated daily from due date until payment, unless NC law caps lower.

---

## 3. SUSPENSION OF SERVICES

Contractor may suspend Services **without liability** after:

1. Invoice **[X]** days past due, **and**
2. Written notice to Client AP and contracts contact, **and**
3. **48 hours** elapsed (or immediately if second consecutive unpaid period)

Suspension does not terminate SOW unless Contractor elects termination under MSA §4.3.

---

## 4. ACCELERATION

Upon failure to cure within **fifteen (15) days** of written demand, **all unpaid Fees** under **all SOWs** become immediately due.

---

## 5. COLLECTION COSTS

Client shall pay **reasonable costs** of collection, including:

- Bank wire fees
- Credit report / skip trace (commercial)
- Mediation fees (if used)
- **Attorneys' fees and court costs** (prevailing party per MSA §11.4)

---

## 6. NO MECHANIC'S LIEN REPRESENTATION

Contractor acknowledges professional services may **not** qualify for mechanic's lien under N.C. Gen. Stat. Chapter 44A. Contractor's remedies are **contractual and judgment-based**, not lien-based.

---

## 7. GOVERNING LAW / VENUE

Enforcement in **Burke County, North Carolina** state court or **U.S. District Court, Western District of North Carolina**. Client waives objection to venue for payment enforcement actions.

---

## 8. ATTORNEY FEES (N.C.G.S. § 6-21.8)

The Parties agree that this is a **business contract** and the **prevailing party** in any action to enforce payment shall recover **reasonable attorneys' fees** from the non-prevailing party.

---

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY OF DISPUTE

Payment disputes may be disclosed to counsel, accountants, insurers, and courts; otherwise Parties shall not publicize dispute except as required by law.

---

## 10. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

Client acknowledges Contractor is a **small business** dependent on timely cash flow and that delayed payment causes **irreparable harm** to Contractor's operations, supporting expedited relief for undisputed amounts.
