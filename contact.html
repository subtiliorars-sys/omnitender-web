Common questions

General information only — not a rate quote or approval guarantee.

How do I get a savings estimate?

Upload or email a recent processing statement to support@omnitender.us, or use the savings calculator for an illustrative comparison. Actual pricing depends on your business type, volume, and equipment.

What is the merchant application?

The online application collects basic business details so we can route you to the right partner program. It takes about 10–15 minutes and does not obligate you to switch.

Do you support crypto or EBT?

We help merchants explore card, crypto, and EBT options where partner programs allow. Final eligibility and setup are confirmed during onboarding — not on this website.

How fast will someone respond?

We aim to reply within one business day for web and email inquiries. For urgent help, call (828) 413-0859.
