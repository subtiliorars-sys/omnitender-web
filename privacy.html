Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Scope: This policy currently covers OmniTender's SMS/messaging program only. A separate privacy notice will govern any payment or financial services OmniTender may offer in the future.

This Privacy Policy explains how OmniTender ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects your information in connection with our SMS text message program.

Information we collect

We collect the information you provide when you sign up, which may include your name and mobile phone number, and a record of your consent to receive text messages.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to send the operational and promotional text messages you opted in to receive, to respond to your requests (such as HELP), and to honor opt-out (STOP) requests. We do not use it for any other purpose.

How we share your information

We do not sell or rent your information. We share it only with service providers as needed to operate our services (such as our messaging platform to deliver text messages), and as required by law. No mobile information (including your phone number) will be shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Your choices

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data retention & security

We retain your information only as long as needed to operate the messaging program and meet legal obligations, and we take reasonable measures to protect it.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@omnitender.us or (828) 413-0859.
