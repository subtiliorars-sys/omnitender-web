Payment types

One platform. Every payment your customers expect.

Licensed partner rails handle settlement; OmniTender handles onboarding, POS, and support.

Credit

Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and the rest — wholesale-style visibility on licensed rails instead of mystery tiered buckets.

Debit

PIN and signature debit on the same terminal as credit — one counter, one relationship, one statement that makes sense.

Stablecoin (USDT/USDC)

Customers pay in stablecoin; you receive USD. Runs beside your existing card terminal — volatility stays on our side.

EBT

Electronic Benefits Transfer for FNS-authorized retailers. We help with enrollment — authorization is granted to the merchant, not OmniTender.

SNAP

Reach SNAP households at the same register as cards. Separate tender type, same checkout flow — when your store is FNS-ready.

Point of sale

Checkout flows matched to how you actually sell — counter, mobile, or line-busting without a six-month rip-and-replace project.

Hardware

Cloud-connected terminals matched to your counter. Hardware typically ships within 48 hours of partner approval.
