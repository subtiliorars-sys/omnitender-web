The problem

Card fees are a silent business partner. One you never hired.

Every card swipe quietly hands 2–4% of your sale to a processor. Do nothing differently for a year and that partner pockets a months-long vacation on your dime.

We don't ask you to fire your card processor. We ask you to stop giving them every single transaction. Add a crypto rail beside the card rail, post your pricing honestly, and watch the fee column on your statement get embarrassingly small.

How it works →

No Competition with Cards

Zero Processing Fees

How it works

From crypto to USD in three unremarkable steps

No new hardware philosophy — once approved, your terminal is live in under 24 hours. Card flow stays exactly as it is today.

01

Customer taps, picks crypto

At checkout your customer chooses crypto on the OmniTender terminal. Your card flow stays exactly as it is. Nobody retrains, nothing gets unplugged, and the line keeps moving.

02

We convert it before you can blink

Crypto converts automatically at the moment of sale. No charts to watch, no wallet apps to babysit, no praying the price holds until Thursday. Volatility is our problem, not yours.

03

Dollars land in your bank

Settlement arrives in plain US dollars, with zero processing fees on the crypto side. The amount on the receipt is the amount in your account. Revolutionary concept, we know.
