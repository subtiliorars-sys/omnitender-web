Burke County · Western North Carolina

Local merchants deserve local support.

OmniTender Systems LLC is based in Morganton, NC — helping independent businesses across Burke County and Western NC accept more payment types with transparent pricing and a direct line to our team.

Rooted here

We are not a distant call center. When you work with OmniTender, you reach people who know the region — from downtown Morganton to surrounding Burke County communities and neighboring Western NC counties.

Processing and settlement run on licensed partner rails; OmniTender never holds merchant or customer funds. We handle onboarding, configuration, and ongoing support so you can focus on running your business.

Who we work with locally

  • Restaurants, cafés, and food trucks along Main Street and beyond
  • Retail shops and boutiques with seasonal traffic
  • Service businesses and trades that need reliable checkout
  • Grocers and markets exploring EBT/SNAP (with merchant FNS authorization)

What you can expect

  • Direct support — call (828) 413-0859 or email support@omnitender.us
  • Payment breadth — cards, debit, stablecoin, and EBT options where partner programs allow
  • Transparent pricing — we walk through your statement before you commit to anything
  • No-pressure onboarding — free rate analysis first; apply only when you're ready

We do not publish merchant counts, volume figures, or savings guarantees on this page. Any comparison or estimate comes from your actual processing statement during a rate analysis — not from marketing claims.
