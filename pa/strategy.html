What we're trying to accomplish

OmniTender Systems LLC · Internal strategy · June 2026

Mission in one line: Help local merchants accept every way their customers already pay — cards, debit, crypto — at lower cost than legacy processors, while OmniTender earns fair residuals and builds software that makes the whole stack easier to use.

Why we're doing this

  • Community service — connect people to payment methods they've already funded (card, benefits, digital wallets).
  • Merchants lose 2–4% to opaque tiered pricing; underserved areas get ignored by big ISOs.
  • We can beat flat-rate apps (Square, generic Stripe checkout) with transparent interchange-plus or compliant dual-pricing — merchants save; we still earn margin on residuals.
  • Wrapper software (OmniVerse, apply funnel, onboarding) is our moat — Daniel can co-build integrations with partners; that's a feature, not a blocker.

Important clarification — Wispr Flow

Wispr Flow is not a payment processor. It's a voice-dictation app (speech-to-text). It uses Stripe to bill its own subscription — it doesn't process merchant card payments.

When we say "beat the competition," we mean processors like legacy ISOs, overpriced tiered accounts, and weak flat-rate platforms — not Wispr Flow.

Our model (how we stay lawful without our own license yet)

  1. OmniTender = sales, onboarding, support, software.
  2. Licensed partners = processing, settlement, compliance rails.
  3. We never hold merchant or customer funds.
  4. Daniel approves every merchant before submission to a processor.

This lets us launch now and negotiate better partner deals as volume grows — then optionally pursue embedded PayFac (Stax Connect, Stripe Connect) when revenue justifies it.

How we win on rates AND margins (both at once)

  • Interchange-plus or compliant dual-pricing — merchant sees real cost + small transparent margin; not bloated tiered "qualified/mid/non" games.
  • Agent residuals — PaymentCloud pays OmniTender ongoing share; we're aligned with merchant retention.
  • Crypto at ~0.5% vs card ~2% effective — huge savings for merchants who want it; we still earn on spread/partner rev share.
  • Software wrapper — faster onboarding, fewer support tickets, higher conversion from ads → apply.html → live merchant.

Software we build (or co-build with partners)

  • omnitender-web — apply.html funnel, ads landing, SMS opt-in.
  • OmniVerse — lead alerts, Q&A, merchant comms.
  • Future — CRM dashboard, statement analyzer, embedded checkout via Stax/Stripe Connect APIs.

Daniel's pitch to partners: "We bring merchants and a software layer; you bring the license and rails. Let's integrate APIs and revenue-share."
