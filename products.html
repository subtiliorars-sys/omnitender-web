Before you choose a program

General information only — not a quote, approval, or equipment guarantee.

Who actually settles my payments?

Licensed partner processors and acquirers handle card settlement and compliance. OmniTender helps you apply, compare programs, and get support — we are not the bank or processor of record.

Can I keep my current terminal?

Often yes, depending on partner and equipment compatibility. Final hardware and migration steps are confirmed during onboarding, not on this page.

Are crypto or EBT available for every business?

Eligibility depends on business type, location, and partner program rules. We help you explore options; approval is confirmed in the application process.
