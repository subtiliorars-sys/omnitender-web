About us

Every Payment. Every Merchant. Every Time.

OmniTender Systems LLC helps independent operators accept more payment types with transparent pricing and direct human support — rooted in Western North Carolina.

What we do

We are a payment services company — not procurement software. We onboard merchants, configure POS and checkout flows, and support you after go-live. Processing and settlement run on licensed partner rails; OmniTender never holds merchant or customer funds.

Who we serve

  • Restaurants, cafés, and food retailers
  • Retail and boutiques with seasonal volume spikes
  • Service businesses and trades that want crypto-capable checkout without volatility risk
  • Grocers and markets pursuing EBT/SNAP (with merchant FNS authorization)

How we're different

  • Transparent pricing — wholesale-style visibility instead of opaque tiers
  • Breadth — cards, debit, crypto, and EBT on one relationship
  • Direct support — reach a real person at (828) 413-0859
  • Local roots — built in Morganton, NC for merchants who can't afford fee leakage

We publish only verified facts on this site. Merchant counts, volume figures, and savings claims appear only when we have permission and documentation to back them up.
